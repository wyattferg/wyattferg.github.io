broken frame

(lyrics and chords)

This is my first single. Recorded in my college bedroom, it's a song about redemption through the way of Jesus. Psalms 139:13 of the Hebrew Bible states "For you formed my inward parts; you knitted me together in my mother's womb." The Creator of the Universe is intentional and intimate.

Furthermore, Psalms 103:14 of the Hebrew Bible states "For He knows our frame; He remembers that we are dust." We are all broken, finite people by nature, and in many ways we will always go against that which is good. Even still, our Creator pursues us with His only Son, Jesus.
