bio

Howdy! My name is Wyatt James Ferguson. I'm just a guy who loves all kinds of art, problem-solving, and learning. I'm originally from Northwest Houston, but am currently a student at Texas A&M University studying Computer Science.

I'm also just a guy who claims to be burdened with thought. Through the making and consuming of music, film, animation, stories, and essays, I seek to externalize this burden. My first single is below.

broken frame

(lyrics and chords)

This is my first single. Recorded in my college bedroom, it's a song about redemption through Jesus, the Christ. Psalm 139:13 of the Bible states "For you formed my inward parts; you knitted me together in my mother's womb." The Creator of the Universe is intentional and intimate.

Furthermore, Psalm 103:14 of the Bible states "For He knows our frame; He remembers that we are dust." We are all broken, finite people by nature, and we will always go against the infinite goodness of our Creator. Yet, our Creator chooses to pursue us, pursue you, with His only Son, Jesus.
